### About Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was a distinguished scientist, visionary leader, and statesman who played a pivotal role in India's missile development programs. He served as the President of India from 2002 to 2007 and left an indelible mark on the nation through his dedication to education, technology, and youth empowerment.
